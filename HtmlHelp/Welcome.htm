Welcome to BridgeLink™

BridgeLink is an extensible suite of integrated bridge engineering software tools.

 

BridgeLink is developed by the Washington State Department of Transportation and is a member of the Alternate Route Project.

Getting Started

By itself, BridgeLink does little more than manage the bridge engineering software components that make up the BridgeLink suite of tools. This document describes how to manage these software tools. Specific information about each tool can be found in the documentation provided with those tools.

A Bit of History

For many years, WSDOT published PGSuper as a stand alone precast-prestressed concrete bridge design application. Unknown to everyone but the development team was that PGSuper was the precursor to BridgeLink. Early on, PGSuper, Library Manager, and TOGA were the only applications to plug into the environment and they were all related to precast-prestressed concrete. Now that there are many plug-ins covering a wide variety of engineering capabilities, it doesn't make sense to refer to them as for a precast-prestressed concrete software.

 

BridgeLink is a more fitting name. It captures the idea that the software tools in the suite are related to bridge engineering and these tools link together to form an integrated suite of tools.
